About Me

I am a Mother, Daughter, Nurse, Midwife and Operational Research Consultant. In my spare time I am an Arbonne Consultant and provide educational opportunities for hard to reach groups.

The knowledge I have gained through this Bootcamp to date has given me the confidence to support a small group of sixth formers who are interested in learning to code.

Becoming a fully fledged Full-Stack coder will give the skills to code raspberry Pi and build tech for fun and improve the user experience and patient pathways through their healthcare journey.
